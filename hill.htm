CRYPTOGRAPHY  
 

Hill Cipher

Here we will selectively review classical cryptosystems. These permutation-based codes include shift, affine, substitution, and transportation ciphers. We have included extensive looks at each of these four cryptosystems, touching on categories such as a basic description and history of the cipher, the advantages and disadvantages of using the cipher, and examples to demonstrate how the encryption and decryption works for each type.

Along with the written elements, we have also included interactive demonstrations of the four-cryptosystem types. The JavaScript-coded demos should help exemplify the advantages and disadvantages of each cipher.
